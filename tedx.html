About The Event

What is TEDxDITU?

TEDxDITU is an independently organised TEDx Event hosted by DIT University. The theme for this year is Dare to dream, Unveiling mysteries to broaden our minds. The aim of the event is to bring light to our local enigmas.

EVENT SPEAKERS

Here are our speakers

Vinay Solanki

Head IoT @ Lenovo (APAC & MEA) | Founder IoT-NCR | IIM-A | Startup Mentor |

Aligned with his passion Vinay Solanki has been extensively involved with IoT, AI, and FinTech in the industry and has authored numerous papers and blogs on the same. He heads IoT ( Internet of Things) at Lenovo, is a recognized top 20 global IoT thought leader and founder of IoT-NCR which is the world's 4th largest IoT community.

Rahul Tyagi

Co-Founder, Lucideus | Ethical Hacker | Cyber security specialist

Rahul Tyagi is one of the most renowned ethical hackers of India. Being a cyber space researcher himself, rahul can claim to have found critical vulnerabilities on websites of Intel, Sony, HP, Discovery Networks, TED and many more. He is also listed in BlackBerry Hall of Fame for his contributions to the organization. A Published Author of two books - Hacking Crux 1 & 2 - both on information security. He has been covered extensively by the global media and is one of the most followed Indian Information Security Expert on various social media platforms.

Nisha Narayanan

COO & Director, RED FM & Magic FM

Entrepreneur and an experienced leader with a proven track record of close to two and half decades in broadcast media industry. Passion and customer driven focus on Alliances & Channels, P&L, Innovation, Creativity, Strategic Growth & events, and Operations. Member of the core committee of the FICCI Radio Forum since 2008. SVP with AROI (Association of Radio Operators for India). As an opinion leader, she has authored several articles on radio policy and regulation and also writes regularly for mainstream media as well as specialist journals.

Ashley Myers

Founder & CEO;The Training room | Drug addiction specialist

Ashley Myers is having a wealth of experience in implementing learning & development initiatives for institutions, government agencies and corporations in the European Nation and United Kingdom. He has lectured in the UK at post-graduate level for the last two years. Ashley has a passion for creating a performance-driven culture ensuring all delegates attending his training sessions develop personal ownership and the leadership skills necessary to succeed in their endeavors.Ashley motivates his students to think on their own feet and aids them with techniques and tools to refine themselves. Ashley brings world a global experience with a fresh approach.

Manish K Tyagi

Commander navy (Retd), 10 TEDx & JOSH Talks speaker / Stand-Up Comic

Manish K Tyagi used to be a Commander in the Indian Navy before he decided to become a stand up comic sometime in 2014. His style is full of unprecedented stories with fun and humour. He is an aspiring model and actor and has till date, one digital ad & 2 web series under his belt. He has also performed at several Naval establishments and goes by the name "The Knotty Commander".

Pragya Aggarwal

Uttarakhand State Powerlifting Champion | Computer Science Engineer | Outreach & International Linkages Officer

Shyamlal Yadav

Member at International Consortium of Investigative Journalists | Sr Editor at The Indian Express

With experience of more than a decade in the field of Journalism, he's an enthusiastic learner and a passionate trainer. for 8 years he was in "India Today" as a senior Editor(Hindi) he supervised all responsibilities related to news planning, assigning, editing, designing etc. And he did several spot reports as well and several impact making, special stories for India Today (English) as well and than he switched to "Indian Express" as a senior editor in Investigative Team.

Neha Shalini Dua

President of Neha foundation working under Beti Bachao | Member of Malviye Mission

Neha shalini dua used to be a model/actress before she decided to work for youth. She is a Youth icon of our new India and she is changing the society with new thoughts and new ideas that will influence people. Her goal is to bring together bright minds to give talks that are idea-focused, and on a wide range of subjects, to foster learning, inspiration and wonder and provoke conversations that matter.
